A B O U T

Chernival 2019 is a series of event which consists of National Olympiad of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC) 2019, Indonesia Chemical Engineering Car Competition (ICECC) 2019, Indonesia Chemical Engineering Paper Competition (ICEPC) 2019, and Closing Event. Chernival is the biggest agenda of the Chemical Engineering Department FTI-ITS. With the goal to participate in humanity, we hope to fulfill it by holding series of events on Chernival 2019.

E V E N T S

National Olympiad of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC)

National Olympiad of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC) is one of the biggest competition in Indonesia which is held for senior high school students who are curious about Chemical Engineering and want to challenge themselves to aim higher. This competition consist of 4 rounds; Preliminary Round 1 (online), Preliminary Round 2 (online), Semi-Final and Final which will be held in our department. Not only competition, but here in NOPEC we also provide Company Visit and Lab Tour. We dare you to join us on NOPEC 2019!

Discover More

Indonesia Chem-E Car Competition (ICECC)

Indonesia Chemical Engineering Car Competition is the one and only Chem-E Car Competition held by university in Indonesia. The objective of this competition is to create car prototype that could be run by chemical reaction through the specified distance while carrying specified load. And also poster presentation competition and many more additional competition. We also provide city tour and Gala Dinner. So do you dare to join us on ICECC 2019?

Discover More

Indonesia Chemical Engineering Paper Competition (ICEPC)

ICEPC (Indonesia Chemical Engineering Paper Competition), a competition about research and innovation at the scale for all universities worldwide in the region of Biotechnology. ICEPC 2019 raises the theme of "Intensified Bio-based Technology towards Global Challenge" with subthemes Bio-process and Bio-product. Are you the one who concerns in solving industrial waste problems? Share your ideas by joining Indonesia Chemical Engineering Paper Competition (ICEPC) 2019.
